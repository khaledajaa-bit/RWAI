The Vault

A vault, not a promise.

Most "RWA" tokens are IOUs pointing at a spreadsheet. Voltex is different: every token in circulation is discharged from a real asset sitting in a custodied vault, verified on a schedule, and provable on-chain in real time.

⚡

Charge before mint

An asset is deposited and verified before a single token is charged into circulation — never the other way around.

🔒

Custodied, not custom

Assets sit with regulated third-party custodians. Voltex never touches the underlying directly.

📡

Provable, always

Attestations publish on-chain on a fixed cadence, so backing is checkable, not just claimed.

🤖

Agent-ready

Built for the Virtuals.io ecosystem — autonomous agents can hold, route, and settle in $VOLTEX natively.

How It Works

From physical asset to charged token.

A fixed four-step circuit. Every unit of $VOLTEX in circulation has passed through all four.

01

Deposit

A real-world asset — property, treasury bill, commodity, or credit note — is placed with a licensed custodian.

02

Verify

Independent auditors confirm ownership, valuation, and legal title before anything is charged.

03

Charge

The vault mints a 1:1 backed token representing a live claim on the underlying asset.

04

Circulate

Tokens move freely across wallets and agents, redeemable back to the vault at any time.
